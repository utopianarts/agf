Amrit Gurung

Amrit Gurung is a Nepalese born cinematographer residing in the UK. He used to sketch and paint in his childhood later developed love for photography. He has worked as a camerman in documentaries and also created music videos for various artists. Amrit has worked as a DP in latest feature film called GURKHA: beneath the bravery.
